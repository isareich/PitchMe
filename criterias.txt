* Clarity: Is the speech easy to understand and follow?
* Conciseness: Does the speech avoid unnecessary jargon and get straight to the point?
* Engagement: Does the speech capture and maintain the audience's attention?
* Credibility: Does the speaker present themselves and their idea in a believable and trustworthy manner?
* Persuasiveness: Does the speech effectively convince the audience of the value proposition?
* Structure: Is the speech well-organized with a clear beginning, middle, and end?
* Delivery: Does the speaker project confidence, enthusiasm, and maintain appropriate eye contact? (This will be assessed based on the provided text, focusing on language that conveys these qualities).
